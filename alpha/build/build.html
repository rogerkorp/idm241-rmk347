Alpha Build

Triggers

When loaded into the website, the user will use a mouse to click on a button. Alternatively, they may hold down on the click to delay the interaction.

Rules

  • Button shrinks down a few pixels, as if it's being pressed down. Shrink state remains on hold. Button color fades in
  • On release, button grows to be a bit larger than normal state, but then settles down to normal size – like a recoil. Color fades out, back to border.
  • Script picks a random adjective and a random noun, and combines the two.
